About us

Dabaga Secondary School is a secondary school in Tanzania with The National Examinations Council of Tanzania (NECTA) registration number S2805

Mission

To become a leading Centre of Intellectual Wealth spearheading the Quest for Sustainable and Inclusive Development.

Vission

The DABAGA SECONDARY SCHOOL will advance the economic, social and technological development of Tanzania and beyond through excellent teaching and learning, research and knowledge exchange.
